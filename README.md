## Hi, I'm Margot 👋

For 20 years I've worked on one question: how do we build AI that genuinely serves people? 

It took me from **DARPA-funded research** on conversational agents in clinical settings (PhD + postdoc, USC Institute for Creative Technologies) to **architecting solutions at scale** at Algolia and Twilio.
